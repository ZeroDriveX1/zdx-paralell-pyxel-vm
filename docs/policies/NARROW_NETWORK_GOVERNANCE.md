# Narrow Network Governance Policy

## Scope

This policy defines a narrow operational responsibility of ZeroDriveX LLC when interacting with authorized government or law enforcement entities.

This policy does not define network governance authority, creator privileges, or control over normal Open-Pyxel operation.

## Limited Authority

ZeroDriveX LLC may act unilaterally only for matters involving government or law enforcement interaction where legal, regulatory, or compliance obligations apply.

This limited scope exists solely for:

- responding to lawful requests,
- fulfilling applicable legal obligations,
- coordinating required compliance actions,
- handling legally required procedures.

## Separation From Network Operation

This authority does not extend to:

- protocol control,
- compute allocation,
- participant advantage,
- governance decisions,
- reputation manipulation,
- changing decentralized network rules,
- granting creator privileges.

Normal Open-Pyxel operation remains governed by protocol rules and established governance processes.

## Request Handling Principles

Government or law enforcement interactions should be handled according to applicable legal requirements and internal integrity standards.

Where permitted, ZeroDriveX LLC will seek to maintain:

- appropriate documentation,
- limited scope,
- security of participant information,
- preservation of network integrity.

## Principle

The purpose of this exception is responsible legal operation, not expanded network authority.

Any matter outside government or law enforcement interaction remains subject to normal Open-Pyxel governance and protocol processes.
